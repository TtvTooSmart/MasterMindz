What age groups are suitable for your online classes?

Our online classes are designed to cater to a wide range of age groups, typically for kids aged between 4 to 14.

Do I need any prior knowledge or experience in the subject to enroll my child?

No prior knowledge or experience is required for most of our beginner-level courses. Our instructors are skilled at teaching students at various skill levels, from beginners to advanced learners.

How are the online classes conducted?

Our online classes are conducted through a free online platform that allows students to join live interactive sessions with our experienced instructors. We will let you know the details of this platform during the free trial.

What equipment or software do we need for the classes?

For most classes, a computer or tablet with a stable internet connection and a webcam is sufficient. In some cases, specific software or tools may be required, and such requirements will be communicated in advance, allowing you to prepare accordingly.

Are the classes recorded for later reference?

While live classes are not typically recorded, we provide course materials, resources, and assignments for students to refer to and review after the class. This ensures that students have access to the necessary materials to reinforce their learning.

How qualified are your instructors?

Our instructors are highly qualified and experienced in their respective fields. They undergo a rigorous selection process and training that’s necessary to deliver effective and engaging online lessons. We ensure that our instructors are skilled at catering to the needs of students of various skill levels.

How do you track the progress of students?

We follow the progress of students through various means, including monthly assessments, weekly assignments, and regular interaction with the instructors. In addition, we provide feedback and personalized guidance to students to help them further enhance their skills.

What is the duration of each class or course?

The duration of each class or course varies depending on the subject and the level of instruction. Typically, classes range from 45 minutes to 1 hour, while courses can span several weeks or months. Specific details regarding the duration will be provided during the free trial.

Are there any exams or assessments for the courses?

Some courses may include periodic assessments or exams to evaluate the progress and understanding of students. However, the focus is on the overall learning experience and practical application of knowledge rather than rigid examinations.

How can I enroll my child in a course?

To enroll your child in one of our courses, simply visit our website, browse the available courses, and follow the enrollment instructions provided. If you have any specific questions or require assistance, our customer support team will be happy to assist you throughout the enrollment process.
