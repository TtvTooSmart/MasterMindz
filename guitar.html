About the course


Discover the World of Guitar: Join our online classes designed for aspiring young musicians who want to learn chords, guitar theory, and play their favorite songs! Our comprehensive course provides a step-by-step teaching method that focuses on developing essential skills such as chord transitioning, posture, fingerpicking, and muscle memory. With interactive live sessions, our expert faculty offers personalized attention and feedback, ensuring steady progress. After just 3 months, your child will have a solid foundation in guitar playing and the ability to confidently play their favorite tunes.
