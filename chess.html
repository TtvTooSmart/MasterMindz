About the course
Unleash the Power of Chess: Join our online classes designed for aspiring young minds who want to learn the art of chess and develop their strategic thinking abilities! Our comprehensive course provides a step-by-step teaching method that focuses on building a strong foundation in chess principles, understanding different tactics and strategies, and honing critical thinking skills. Through interactive live sessions, our expert faculty offers personalized attention and feedback, ensuring continuous improvement. By the end of this course, your child will have gained confidence in their chess abilities and developed a passion for this timeless game.

  • STRATEGIC GENIUS

    By the conclusion of this course, students will demonstrate enhanced strategic thinking skills and the ability to plan and execute effective chess moves.

  • TECHNICAL PROFICIENCY

    Gain expertise in chess openings, mid-game strategies, endgame techniques, and tactical maneuvers.

  • WEEKLY HOMEWORK

  • MONTHLY ANALYSIS AND TOURNAMENTS

  • CERTIFICATION
